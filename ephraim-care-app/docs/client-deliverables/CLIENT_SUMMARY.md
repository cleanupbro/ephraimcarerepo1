## What We Built

### Website Pages

| Page | Purpose | Status |
|------|---------|--------|
| Homepage | Main landing page with hero, services overview | LIVE |
| About | Company information, values, commitment | LIVE |
| Services | List of 8 NDIS services offered | LIVE |
| FAQ | Frequently asked questions with NDIS logos | LIVE |
| Referrals | Online referral form for new participants | LIVE |
| Contact | Contact form and business information | LIVE |

### Key Features Implemented

1. **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
2. **NDIS Branding** - "I Love NDIS" and Registered Provider badges
3. **Contact Forms** - Integrated with n8n webhooks
4. **Telegram Notifications** - Instant alerts for new submissions
5. **Admin Dashboard** - Manage referrals and contacts
6. **Service Map** - Shows coverage areas across Western Sydney
