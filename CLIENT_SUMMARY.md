## What We Built

### Public Website (8 Pages)
1. **Homepage** — Hero, services overview, testimonials, CTAs
2. **About** — Company story, team, mission
3. **Services** — Grid of 8 NDIS services
4. **Service Detail Pages** (8) — Individual service pages
5. **Contact** — Contact form with Telegram notifications
6. **Referrals** — NDIS referral form with Supabase storage
7. **FAQ** — Common questions with accordion
8. **Complaints** — Feedback and complaints form

### Admin Dashboard (10 Pages)
1. **Dashboard** — Stats overview, recent activity
2. **Referrals** — View and manage referrals
3. **Contacts** — View contact form submissions
4. **Participants** — Participant management
5. **Appointments** — Appointment scheduling
6. **Incidents** — Incident report management
7. **Handovers** — Shift handover records
8. **Staff** — Staff member management
9. **Analytics** — (Planned)
10. **Settings** — (Planned)

### Features
- Mobile-responsive design
- Telegram notifications for new referrals/contacts
- Supabase database integration
- NDIS compliance (correct terminology)
- Admin authentication with Supabase Auth
- Auto-deploy from GitHub to Vercel
